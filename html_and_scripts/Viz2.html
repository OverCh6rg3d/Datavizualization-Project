Dubai Accident Data Analysis

Interactive dashboard comparing Weekday vs. Weekend trends

How to use:

  • Line Chart: Drag across the chart to filter the time range.
  • Bar/Pie Chart: Click a specific category to filter the trend line.

Data Note:

The trend lines represent the average number of accidents per hour, normalized by the number of specific days (e.g., total Fridays vs. total Mondays) to ensure a fair comparison.
